What exactly is E-Waste?

Electronic waste (also known as E-Waste) are electronic products that have reached the end of their lifecycle or are unwanted. This includes working and broken items that are thrown away or donated to a charity like Oxfam for example.

Often, if the item doesn't sell, it will be thrown away. E-waste is especially hazardous due to toxic chemicals that typically leak from the metals and components inside when buried in landfills.

What impact does it have?

E-Waste has many different impacts, from the harmful effects on the environment to the hazardous chemicals these components contain
